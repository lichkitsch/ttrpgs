# dnduet

## Cover
_dnduet_ is a customizable tabletop RPG for two.

To play _dnduet_ you'll need:

1. A set of dice
2. A game master (GM) with a quest
3. A completed character sheet

## Page 1

2. Max HP at level 1 is 10+`CON`
